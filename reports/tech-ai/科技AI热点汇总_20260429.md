### 1. HERMES.md 计费 Bug：提交消息中一个关键词让你多扣 $200 🔥🔥🔥
**[来源：HN 976🔥 / GitHub / 36氪 / 机器之心]**

- Anthropic 的 AI 客服承认 Bug 存在，但**拒绝退款**
- 复现条件：仅大写 `HERMES.md` 触发，小写/无扩展名/其他文件名均正常
- 社区讽刺："他们号称在构建世界上最先进的 AI，客服策略却比正则匹配还蠢"

### 2. CVE-2026-31431 "Copy Fail"：Linux 内核本地提权漏洞 🔥🔥
**[来源：HN 557🔥 / copy.fail / 腾讯云]**

- 影响几乎所有 Linux 发行版（自 2017 年起存在）
- 攻击者仅需本地账户，**10行 Python** 即可获得 root 权限
- 机制：利用 AF_ALG 加密接口 + splice() 系统调用，向页缓存写入 4 字节可控数据
- 不触发 inotify 等文件系统监控
- 已发布补丁，建议立即更新

### 全文精读（带标注）

Investor interest in Anthropic has reached a **feverish pitch** `[feverish pitch: 狂热顶点——形容热情/兴趣达到最高程度，pitch 这里指程度/强度]`. The maker of the Claude AI assistant has received multiple **preemptive** `[preemptive: 先发制人的/抢先的——指投资者不等公司正式启动融资就主动出价]` offers to raise fresh capital of around $50 billion at a valuation in the $850 billion to $900 billion range, according to half a dozen sources familiar with the matter.

Bloomberg and Business Insider reported earlier this month that Anthropic received multiple **preemptive bids** `[preemptive bids: 抢先出价——PE/VC 术语，指在公司尚未正式启动融资时就递交的投资意向]` at an $800 billion valuation, but at that time, the company had not yet **committed to** `[committed to: 承诺/决定——常见搭配 commit to doing sth]` a fundraise.

What appears to have changed is that **demand has only intensified** `[demand has intensified: 需求只增不减——intensified 比 increased 更有力度]`, **pushing up the price** `[pushing up the price: 推高了价格——这里 price 指估值]`. Multiple sources tell TechCrunch that while Anthropic has not formally kicked off a round, it plans to bring a proposal to its board in May and is now **leaning toward** `[leaning toward: 倾向于——比 considering 更近一步但未最终确认]` accepting offers in the $850 billion to $900 billion range — more than double its valuation of $380 billion from just two months ago.

A funding at that level would push Anthropic past chief rival OpenAI, which closed a $122 billion round in February at a post-money valuation of just over $852 billion. It would also underscore **the breakneck pace** `[breakneck pace: 极快的速度/疯狂的节奏——breakneck 字面意"折断脖子"，形容快得危险]` at which the AI market is evolving. To put the numbers in perspective, Anthropic was valued at $18 billion in late 2023 and at $60 billion in late 2024.

The interest is understandable. Earlier this month, Anthropic said its business has reached an **annualized revenue** `[annualized revenue: 年化收入——将当前月/季的收入乘以12/4得到的全年预估值]` **run rate** `[run rate: 运行率——用当前表现推算的年化数据，VC/PE 常用指标]` of $30 billion, though several of our sources say the run rate is now closer to $40 billion. At the end of 2025, its annualized revenue run rate was roughly $9 billion — meaning its **top line** `[top line: 营收线/收入——与 bottom line（利润）相对]` has roughly **quadrupled** `[quadrupled: 翻了四倍——triple 三倍，quadruple 四倍]` in under five months.

Anthropic has also seen **explosive** `[explosive: 爆炸式的——形容增长极快]` growth in its AI coding products. Claude Code, a **command-line** `[command-line: 命令行——开发者常用的终端交互方式]` coding agent, and Cowork, its **agentic** `[agentic: 具有 Agent 能力的——2025-2026年 AI 领域新造词，形容有自主决策和执行能力的 AI 系统]` coding platform for enterprises, have become the company's **fastest-growing revenue drivers** `[fastest-growing revenue drivers: 增速最快的收入引擎——driver 在商业语境中指"驱动因素"]`. Investors see **considerable runway** `[considerable runway: 可观的发展空间——runway 字面意"跑道"，比喻还有很大增长空间]` for expansion into finance, life sciences, and healthcare.

One institutional investor told TechCrunch it had **earmarked** `[earmarked: 指定用途/预留——财务术语，指专门为某目的划拨资金]` $5 billion for the round but has not yet been granted a meeting with Anthropic's CFO, Krishna Rao — a sign of just how **oversubscribed** `[oversubscribed: 超额认购——投资者想投的金额超过了公司实际融资需求]` the deal is expected to be.

A **person briefed on** `[person briefed on: 知情人/被告知相关情况的人——briefed 意为"被简要通报"]` the company's thinking said Anthropic has **weighed various structures** `[weighed various structures: 权衡各种（融资）结构——weigh 在商务语境中意为"仔细考虑"]` for the round, including a primary issuance of new shares as well as a component that would allow **early employees to sell a portion of their vested equity** `[sell a portion of their vested equity: 出售一部分已归属股权——vested equity 指已满足条件可自由处置的股份]`. **Secondary sales** `[secondary sales: 二级市场交易/老股转让——区别于公司发行新股的 primary]` of Anthropic shares have already been happening at valuations near $800 billion, sources say.

The new round, **if consummated** `[if consummated: 如果最终完成——consummated 比 completed 更正式，常用于交易/合同语境]`, would represent one of the largest private fundraises in history and would **set the stage for** `[set the stage for: 为……铺平道路——set the stage 字面意"搭好舞台"]` what many expect to be an **initial public offering** `[initial public offering (IPO): 首次公开募股]` in the not-too-distant future. Sources say Anthropic has already begun early conversations with bankers about a potential listing as soon as October.

Anthropic **declined to comment** `[declined to comment: 拒绝置评——新闻写作中最常见的表述方式之一]`.

## 📝 学习笔记

### 核心 AI/Tech 词汇
| 单词/短语 | 含义 | 例句语境 |
|-----------|------|----------|
| annualized revenue run rate | 年化收入运行率 | "its business has reached an annualized revenue run rate of $30 billion" |
| agentic | 具有 Agent 能力的（新造词） | "Cowork, its agentic coding platform for enterprises" |
| command-line | 命令行 | "Claude Code, a command-line coding agent" |
| preemptive (bid/offer) | 抢先出价 | "received multiple preemptive offers" |
| oversubscribed | 超额认购 | "how oversubscribed the deal is expected to be" |
| top line | 营收（与 bottom line 利润相对） | "its top line has roughly quadrupled" |
| run rate | 运行率/年化速率 | "the run rate is now closer to $40 billion" |
| post-money valuation | 投后估值 | "a post-money valuation of just over $852 billion" |

### 重要短语/习语
| 短语 | 含义 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| feverish pitch | 狂热顶点 | "interest has reached a feverish pitch" — 描述市场情绪极度高涨 |
| breakneck pace | 极快的速度 | "the breakneck pace at which the AI market is evolving" — 形容变化快得惊人 |
| set the stage for | 为……铺平道路 | "would set the stage for an initial public offering" — 某事件为后续事件创造条件 |
| leaning toward | 倾向于 | "is now leaning toward accepting offers" — 尚未最终决定但已有明确倾向 |
| to put the numbers in perspective | 为这些数字提供参照 | 用于对比当前数字与历史数据 |
| considerable runway | 可观的增长空间 | "investors see considerable runway for expansion" — VC 常用语 |

### 技术写作模式
1. **数据对比增强叙事张力**："valued at $18 billion in late 2023 and at $60 billion in late 2024" → 通过时间线展示估值增长曲线，比直接说"增长了50倍"更有冲击力
2. **匿名信源的标准化表述**："according to half a dozen sources familiar with the matter" / "a person briefed on the company's thinking" — 保护信源的同时暗示信息可靠度
3. **动词选择的精确性**：weigh（权衡）> consider（考虑）> think about（想）— 在财经报道中动词的力度直接反映决策进程

### 语法亮点
1. **倒装强调**："What appears to have changed is that demand has only intensified" — What 引导的名词性从句做主语，强调变化的核心
2. **分词做后置定语**："A person briefed on the company's thinking said..." — 过去分词短语做后置定语，比定语从句 "who was briefed on" 更简洁
3. **虚拟条件**："if consummated" — 省略了 it is/were 的条件状语，正式文体中常见
4. **破折号插入解释**："its CFO, Krishna Rao — a sign of just how oversubscribed..." — 破折号引出对前文的评论性补充
