- **作者**: Alexander Panfilov 等
- **核心**: 自动研究管道使用 LLM 代理**自主发现**白盒对抗攻击算法
- **意义**: AI 自动发现 AI 的漏洞，安全攻防进入新阶段

### ⭐ 3. Why Does Self-Distillation (Sometimes) Degrade LLM Reasoning?
- **作者**: 微软研究院 / KAIST / 首尔国立大学
- **核心**: 自蒸馏会缩短推理链但可能**降低数学推理能力**，特别是分布外任务；与抑制"认识论表达"（不确定性表达）有关
- **意义**: 对蒸馏方法的深刻反思，提醒不确定性表达对泛化的重要性

### ⭐ 4. Natural-Language Agent Harnesses (NLAHs)
- **作者**: Linyue Pan 等
- **核心**: 外部化 AI 代理的自然语言控制逻辑。在 OSWorld 基准上任务成功率 **47.2%**（原生代码 30.4%）

### ⭐ 5. Intern-S1-Pro: 万亿参数科学多模态基础模型
- **作者**: 上海人工智能实验室

### ⭐ 6. Reaching Beyond the Mode: RL for Distributional Reasoning in LLMs
- **核心**: 多答案 RL 让 LLM 在单次推理中生成**多样化合理答案集 + 校准置信度**
- **意义**: 从"找最优答案"到"给出答案分布"，对医学诊断等高不确定性场景意义重大

### ⭐ 7. Trace2Skill: 从代理轨迹中蒸馏可迁移技能

### ⭐ 8. Self-Improvement of LLMs: A Technical Overview

## 原文精读（生词/短语/语法标注版）

**QWEN3.5-OMNI, ALIBABA'S OMNIMODAL `[omnimodal: 全模态的——omni(全部) + modal(模态)，AI领域新造词]` AI, NOW HANDLES VOICE CLONING, 10-HOUR AUDIO, REAL-TIME WEB SEARCH, AND BEATS GEMINI ON AUDIO BENCHMARKS—ALL IN ONE MODEL.**

---

**IN BRIEF** `[in brief: 简而言之——新闻文章常见的摘要标记]`

- Alibaba's Qwen 3.5 Omni brings true real-time omnimodal AI to the **frontier** `[frontier: 前沿——AI领域常说frontier model(前沿模型)]` race.
- **Native** `[native: 原生的——在AI语境中指模型内建的能力，而非通过外部工具拼接]` audio-visual processing beats **stitched** `[stitched: 拼接的——stitch本义为"缝合"，此处形容将多个模块拼接在一起的管道方案]` **multimodal pipelines** `[multimodal pipeline: 多模态处理管道——指将不同模态(文本/音频/图像)分开处理再合并的技术流程]` in speed and **coherence** `[coherence: 连贯性/一致性——重要学术和技术用词]`.
- Voice cloning, **semantic interruption** `[semantic interruption: 语义中断——指AI能理解用户话语的含义来判断是否被打断，而非仅靠音量/停顿]`, and **vibe coding** `[vibe coding: 氛围编码——AI圈新流行语，指让AI通过感知(看/听)来写代码，而非明确的文字指令]` signal a shift toward fully **interactive agents** `[interactive agent: 交互式智能体]`.

---

Alibaba just **dropped** `[drop: (口语)发布/推出——非常地道的俚语用法，= released/launched]` its most **ambitious** `[ambitious: 雄心勃勃的——常见但易忘的形容词]` AI upgrade yet.

The company's Qwen team released Qwen 3.5 Omni on Sunday, a new version of its "omnimodal" AI that **simultaneously** `[simultaneously: 同时地——重要副词，注意发音 /ˌsaɪməlˈteɪniəsli/]` processes text, images, audio, and video, and **talks back** `[talk back: 回嘴/回应——此处指AI能实时语音回应]` in real time across 36 languages, placing its model on the same **battlefield** `[battlefield: 战场——此处比喻AI竞争格局]` as the latest **state-of-the-art** `[state-of-the-art: 最先进的——技术文章高频复合形容词，缩写SOTA]` AI **foundational models** `[foundational model: 基础模型——也叫foundation model，指大规模预训练模型]` currently available.

"Omni" isn't just a **marketing buzzword** `[marketing buzzword: 营销热词——buzzword指流行但可能空洞的时髦词汇]` here. Most AI models you interact with are primarily text-in, text-out systems. Some handle images, some handle voice. Qwen 3.5 Omni handles all of them **natively** `[natively: 原生地]`, at the same time, without the need to convert everything to text through **third-party tools** `[third-party tools: 第三方工具]`.

The new model comes in three sizes—Plus, Flash, and Light—all supporting a small (**by today's standards** `[by today's standards: 以今天的标准来看——常用插入语]`) 256,000-token **context window** `[context window: 上下文窗口——LLM核心概念，指模型一次能处理的token数量]`. It was trained on over 100 million hours of audio-visual data—a **scale** `[scale: 规模——AI领域常说at scale(大规模)]` that puts it in a **different weight class** `[different weight class: 不同量级——来自拳击术语(重量级别)，形容远超竞争对手]` from most competitors.

Qwen 3.5 Omni is an **evolution** `[evolution: 进化/演进——比upgrade更有"渐进发展"之意]` of Qwen 3 Omni Flash, Alibaba's previous omnimodal model released in December 2025. That version already impressed with its ability to process video and audio simultaneously—it could handle image editing instructions combining multiple visual inputs in ways competitors couldn't—and **streamed** `[stream: 流式传输——技术用词，指数据实时持续传输]` voice responses with **latency** `[latency: 延迟——技术核心指标，指从输入到输出的时间差]` as low as 234 **milliseconds** `[millisecond: 毫秒——注意复数形式]`.

It was also the first model to try an alternative to Google's NotebookLM. It achieved something, but the quality was **not on par with** `[not on par with: 达不到…的水平——on par with = 与…同等水平，来自高尔夫术语(par = 标准杆)]` Google's offer.

Qwen 3.5 Omni takes all of that and adds a longer context window, better reasoning, a much wider language library, and a set of real-time interaction features the previous generation didn't have.

The **headline upgrade** `[headline upgrade: 头条级升级/最亮眼的升级——headline作形容词表示"最重要的"]` is what happens when you actually talk to it. Qwen3.5-Omni now supports semantic interruption: It can tell the difference between you saying "**uh-huh**" `[uh-huh: 嗯哼——英语中表示确认/附和的语气词]` mid-sentence and actually wanting to **cut in** `[cut in: 插嘴/打断——重要短语动词]`, so it won't stop **mid-thought** `[mid-thought: 说到一半——mid- 前缀表示"中间的"]` every time someone coughs in the background, making spoken interaction more **seamless** `[seamless: 无缝的/流畅的——技术和日常英语中的高频形容词]`.

A new technique called **ARIA**, short for **Adaptive Rate Interleave Alignment** `[Adaptive Rate Interleave Alignment: 自适应速率交错对齐——AI技术命名法的典型风格]`, also fixes a subtle but **persistent** `[persistent: 持续存在的/顽固的——比continuous更强调"难以消除"]` **annoyance** `[annoyance: 烦恼/令人恼火的事]`: AI systems that **garble** `[garble: 弄乱/念错——指将信息搞混乱，此处指AI朗读时把数字或生词念错]` numbers or unusual words when reading aloud. ARIA dynamically **syncs** `[sync: 同步——synchronize的缩写，技术领域日常用词]` text and speech to keep output natural and accurate.

Then there's **voice cloning** `[voice cloning: 语音克隆——AI用少量语音样本复制某人声音的技术]`. Users can upload a voice sample and have the model **adopt** `[adopt: 采用——此处指AI"采用/模仿"上传的声音]` that voice in its responses, a feature that puts Qwen directly **in competition with** `[in competition with: 与…竞争——常用表达]` ElevenLabs and other **dedicated** `[dedicated: 专门的——此处指专门做语音的工具]` voice tools. We weren't able to access this feature, though, because this is a feature that, **at least for now** `[at least for now: 至少目前来看——常用过渡语]`, is only available **via API** `[via API: 通过API——via = 通过/经由]`.

On **multilingual** `[multilingual: 多语言的——multi(多) + lingual(语言的)]` voice stability **benchmarks** `[benchmark: 基准测试——AI领域核心词汇，指标准化的性能评估测试]`, Qwen3.5 Omni Plus beat ElevenLabs, GPT-Audio, and Minimax across 20 languages. The model also now supports real-time web search, meaning it can answer questions about **breaking news** `[breaking news: 突发新闻]` or live market data without **pretending** `[pretend: 假装——此处暗讽部分AI模型在不知道答案时会编造信息(幻觉)]` it already knows.

The team is also highlighting what they're calling "Audio-Visual Vibe Coding," the model can watch a **screen recording** `[screen recording: 屏幕录制]` or video of a coding task and write **functional code** `[functional code: 可运行的代码——functional此处不是"函数式"而是"能正常工作的"]` based purely on what it sees and hears, **no text prompt required** `[no text prompt required: 无需文字提示——简洁有力的独立短语结构]`. It's a small **preview** `[preview: 预告/预览]` of how AI assistants might eventually operate **inside your workflow rather than alongside it** `[inside your workflow rather than alongside it: 在工作流程内部而非旁边——very insightful distinction，区分"嵌入式AI"和"辅助式AI"]`.

To understand what "omnimodal" actually means **in practice** `[in practice: 在实践中/实际上]`, we ran a quick test: We fed both Qwen3.5-Omni and ChatGPT 5.4 in "thinking" mode the same YouTube Short—a clip of Dastan President and commentator Farokh discussing breaking news. Qwen 3.5 Omni processed the video natively and returned a full analysis in about one minute: who was speaking, what they were discussing, and a **substantive** `[substantive: 实质性的/有内容的——比substantial更强调"有真正价值"]` comment on the topic based on its own knowledge of the subject area.

ChatGPT 5.4, which is not omnimodal, had to manage with what it got. It extracted frames from the video, ran them through a **vision model** `[vision model: 视觉模型]`, used Whisper to **transcribe** `[transcribe: 转录/将语音转为文字——重要动词]` the audio, and applied an **OCR** `[OCR: Optical Character Recognition, 光学字符识别——从图像中提取文字的技术]` tool to read **embedded subtitles** `[embedded subtitles: 内嵌字幕——区别于外挂字幕(external subtitles)]`—three separate processes **stitched together** `[stitch together: 拼接在一起——形象比喻，像缝衣服一样把多个步骤拼起来]` to **approximate** `[approximate: 近似/模拟——此处作动词，= come close to]` what Qwen3.5-Omni does **in a single pass** `[in a single pass: 一次通过/一遍完成——技术用语，强调效率]`. The result took nine minutes, and that's **under ideal conditions** `[under ideal conditions: 在理想条件下——scientific writing中的常见表达]`: a well-lit video with clean audio and **burned-in** `[burned-in: 烧录的/硬编码的——指字幕直接嵌入视频画面]` subtitles. Real-world content rarely offers all three.

In our quick tests across multiple inputs, the model also handled prompts in Spanish, Portuguese, and English without issue—switching languages **mid-conversation** `[mid-conversation: 对话进行中——mid-前缀用法]` without losing context.

On standard benchmarks, Qwen 3.5 Omni Plus **outperformed** `[outperform: 超越/表现优于——常见对比用词]` Gemini 3.1 Pro on general audio understanding, reasoning, and translation tasks, and **matched it on** `[match sb on: 在…方面与某人持平——match此处不是"匹配"而是"比肩"]` audio-visual **comprehension** `[comprehension: 理解能力——AI评估中的核心维度]`. Speech recognition now covers 113 languages and **dialects** `[dialect: 方言——语言学基础词汇]`—up from 19 in the previous generation.

This is Alibaba's second major AI release **in six weeks** `[in six weeks: 六周内——强调发布节奏之快]`. In February, it launched Qwen 3.5, a text-and-vision model that matched or beat frontier models on reasoning and coding benchmarks—part of a **streak** `[streak: 连续成功/一连串——a winning streak = 连胜]` that has also included Qwen Deep Research and **a lineup of** `[a lineup of: 一系列——lineup指"阵容/排列"]` tools **rivaling** `[rival: 与…匹敌/竞争——此处作现在分词表伴随]` OpenAI and Google. Qwen 3.5 Omni **extends that momentum** `[extend that momentum: 延续那股势头——momentum = 动量/势头，商业新闻高频词]` into full multimodal territory, at a time when every major AI lab is **racing to** `[race to: 争相/竞相——形容激烈竞争]` build systems that handle the **full spectrum** `[full spectrum: 全部范围/完整光谱——spectrum从物理概念引申为"范围"]` of human communication—not just words on a screen.

The model is available now via Alibaba Cloud's API and can be tested directly at Qwen Chat or through Hugging Face's online demo.

### 技术写作模式
1. **对比测试法**: 作者直接用 Qwen 和 ChatGPT 做相同任务对比（1分钟 vs 9分钟），让数据说话
2. **括号式定义**: "ARIA, short for Adaptive Rate Interleave Alignment" — 技术文章中引入术语的标准句式
3. **让步转折**: "It achieved something, but the quality was not on par with..." — 先肯定再否定的客观写法
4. **内行暗讽**: "without pretending it already knows" — 暗讽其他模型的幻觉问题，不点名但业内都懂

### 语法亮点
1. **定语从句嵌套**: "a text-and-vision model that matched or beat frontier models on..." — that引导的限定性定语从句
2. **同位语**: "a new version of its 'omnimodal' AI that simultaneously processes..." — 用that从句做进一步说明
3. **独立主格**: "no text prompt required" — 独立结构，相当于 without any text prompt being required
4. **比较级结构**: "a scale that puts it in a different weight class from most competitors" — from引导比较对象
