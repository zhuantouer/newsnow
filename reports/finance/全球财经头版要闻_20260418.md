## 原文精读（含标注）

CAIRO (AP) — The **standoff** `[standoff: 对峙/僵持——常用于描述两方互不退让的紧张局面，military/diplomatic standoff]` over the Strait of Hormuz **escalated** `[escalate: 升级/加剧——"the conflict escalated" 是新闻高频词，注意不及物用法]` again Saturday as Iran **reversed** `[reverse: 逆转/撤回——reverse a decision/policy/reopening]` its reopening of the **crucial waterway** `[crucial waterway: 关键水道——waterway 泛指航运通道，crucial 强调战略重要性]` and **fired on** `[fire on: 向…开火——注意 fire on sb/sth 的固定搭配，不同于 fire at（精度更低的射击）]` ships attempting to pass, **in retaliation for** `[in retaliation for: 作为对…的报复——外交/军事语境高频短语]` the continued US **blockade** `[blockade: 封锁——海上/港口封锁，比 embargo（禁运）更强硬，涉及军事行动]`.

Confusion over the **critical chokepoint** `[chokepoint: 咽喉要道/战略瓶颈点——地缘政治核心术语，霍尔木兹海峡是全球最重要的 oil chokepoint]` threatened to deepen the **energy crisis roiling the global economy** `[roil: 搅乱/使动荡——roiling the economy 比 affecting 更形象，暗示剧烈翻搅。注意 roil vs broil 的区别]` and push the price of oil back toward recent highs.

Iran's military declared the Strait of Hormuz had "**returned to its previous state**" `[returned to its previous state: 恢复到此前状态——外交辞令，实际含义是重新关闭]`. The announcement came after President Trump had said the blockade on Iranian ports would "**remain in full force**" `[remain in full force: 继续全力维持——法律/军事用语，full force 表示不打任何折扣]` — **effectively undermining** `[effectively undermine: 实质上破坏了——effectively 在此不是"有效地"而是"事实上/实质上"]` the goodwill gesture Iran made just a day earlier.

Two **gunboats** `[gunboat: 炮艇——小型武装舰艇，gunboat diplomacy 是"炮舰外交"的经典表达]` from Iran's **Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps** `[IRGC: 伊朗伊斯兰革命卫队——伊朗最精锐军事力量，独立于正规军，直接向最高领袖负责]` **opened fire on** `[open fire on: 向…开火——open fire 比 fire on 更强调"开始射击"的动作]` a tanker **transiting** `[transit: 通过/过境——海事术语，transiting the strait = 通过海峡]` the Strait of Hormuz, the **UKMTO** `[UKMTO: UK Maritime Trade Operations, 英国海事贸易行动中心——负责监控全球商船安全的英国皇家海军机构]` confirmed. India also confirmed two of its **flagged ships** `[flagged ships: 悬挂…国旗的船只——Indian-flagged ships = 在印度注册/悬挂印度国旗的船只，ship's flag 决定其法律管辖权]` were targeted in the attacks.

"Iran **can't blackmail us**," `[blackmail: 勒索/敲诈——通常指以威胁手段迫使对方就范，Trump 用此词暗示伊朗试图以海峡控制权要挟美国]` Trump said in a post on Truth Social, adding that the US was **preparing to board and seize** `[board and seize: 登船并扣押——国际海事执法行动的标准用语，boarding = 强制登船检查]` **Iranian-linked vessels** `[Iranian-linked vessels: 与伊朗有关联的船只——不仅限于伊朗籍船只，包括为伊朗运货的第三国船只]` to **pile pressure on** `[pile pressure on: 对…施加压力——pile 比 put 更强调"层层加码"的力度]` Tehran and force the reopening of the strait.

The **dramatic reversal** `[dramatic reversal: 戏剧性逆转——新闻写作常用表达，dramatic 不是"戏剧的"而是"出乎意料的/剧烈的"]` came just hours after global markets had celebrated Iran's reopening of the waterway on Friday. Oil prices had **plummeted** `[plummet: 暴跌/直线下坠——比 fall/drop 更强烈，暗示自由落体般的下跌]` more than 11% — with **Brent crude** `[Brent crude: 布伦特原油——全球石油定价基准，产自北海；与 WTI（西德克萨斯中质原油，美国基准）相对]`, the global **benchmark** `[benchmark: 基准——金融/商品领域的核心术语，benchmark rate（基准利率），benchmark index（基准指数）]`, falling to just below $88.25 per barrel, and **WTI** `[WTI: West Texas Intermediate, 西德克萨斯中质原油——美国石油定价基准]` sinking to $81.20 per barrel. The **whiplash** `[whiplash: 鞭打/猛烈反转——本义是颈部扭伤（如车祸中），此处引申为市场情绪的"急转弯"，极其形象]` in markets was expected to **intensify** `[intensify: 加剧/强化——the crisis intensified 是新闻标准用法]` when trading resumes Monday, as **traders brace for** `[brace for: 为…做好（不好的事情的）准备——brace yourself = 绷紧身体/做好准备，带有紧张感]` a potential **surge** `[surge: 激增/飙升——oil price surge, 对应中文"暴涨"]` in oil prices that could **erase** `[erase: 抹去——erase gains/losses 是金融报道的经典搭配]` Friday's dramatic gains.

**In normal times**, 20 percent of the world's oil supplies **transit through** `[transit through: 经由…运输——介词 through 强调穿越]` the strait, but this had **slowed to a trickle** `[slow to a trickle: 减缓至涓涓细流——trickle 本义细流/滴水，此处形容贸易量大幅萎缩，极其vivid]` as Iran **selectively allowed** `[selectively allow: 选择性允许——暗示伊朗在挑选哪些船能过、哪些不能]` ships to pass, causing **soaring prices** of oil, gas, and other **commodities** `[commodities: 大宗商品——包括石油、天然气、金属、农产品等在交易所交易的标准化商品]`.

The **IMF-World Bank spring meetings** `[spring meetings: 春季年会——IMF和世界银行每年4月在华盛顿举行的全球经济政策年度峰会]` in Washington, which **wrapped up** `[wrap up: 结束/收尾——wrap up a meeting/conference]` Saturday, had been **dominated by** `[be dominated by: 被…主导——被动语态，强调某一议题压倒性地占据了会议焦点]` anxieties over the Iran conflict. CNBC reported that more than 30 central bankers and policymakers described the war as the **foremost risk** `[foremost risk: 首要风险——foremost = first and most important]` to the global economy, with one **attendee** `[attendee: 与会者/参加者——比 participant 更正式]` describing the atmosphere as **"a twilight zone"** `[twilight zone: 暮光地带/模糊不确定的境地——借用经典美剧名，形容处于光明与黑暗之间的不确定状态]`.

The **ceasefire** `[ceasefire: 停火——cease（停止）+ fire（开火），比 truce（休战）更正式]` between Israel and Lebanon, **brokered by** `[broker: 斡旋/居间促成——broker a deal/ceasefire/peace, 名词形式也有"经纪人"之意]` the Trump administration, was set to **expire** `[expire: 到期/届满——ceasefire expires, contract expires, visa expires]` on April 22. But Saturday's **escalation** `[escalation: 升级——the latest escalation 是地缘政治报道的标配词]` cast fresh **doubt** over whether it — or any broader peace deal — could **hold** `[hold: 维持/保持有效——will the ceasefire hold? 是经典新闻句式，hold 在此≠"握住"]`.

### 修辞与写作技巧

1. **反转叙事结构（Reversal Narrative）**：文章以"standoff...escalated again"开篇，立即揭示冲突升级；然后用"dramatic reversal"回顾周五的乐观，形成强烈对比。这种"先给结果，再给反差"的手法让读者瞬间抓住新闻核心。

2. **拟物隐喻（Vivid Metaphor）**："whiplash" 原意是车祸中的颈部扭伤，用来形容市场情绪的猛烈反转——从狂喜到恐惧的急速摆动。"slowed to a trickle" 将全球石油贸易比作细水涓涓，画面感极强。

3. **层层递进的动词选择**：plummet > drop > fall > decline——AP 选用 plummeted 描述油价下跌，sinking 描述 WTI，raced 描述股市上涨——每个动词都精准匹配了幅度和情绪。

4. **"effectively" 的妙用**："effectively undermining the goodwill gesture" 中 effectively 不是"有效地"，而是"事实上"——Trump 的言论虽然不是直接命令关闭海峡，但事实效果等同于破坏了伊朗的善意姿态。这种委婉的因果归因是严肃新闻写作的标志。

5. **短句制造紧迫感**：全文多处使用短句（"Iran can't blackmail us," Trump said）穿插长分析段落，节奏张弛有度，让读者在密集信息中保持注意力。

### 语法亮点

1. **分词短语作状语**："The standoff over the Strait of Hormuz escalated again Saturday **as Iran reversed** its reopening of the crucial waterway **and fired on** ships **attempting to pass**..." —— 三个动作（reverse, fire, attempt）通过 as 引导的时间状语从句和现在分词短语 (attempting to pass) 紧凑串联，一句话交代了全部关键信息。

2. **独立主格结构暗含因果**："**Confusion over the critical chokepoint** threatened to deepen the energy crisis..." —— 名词短语 + 动词的结构，confusion 并非主语从句而是以名词短语直接引出后果，简洁有力。

3. **引语嵌入主句**：'"Iran can't blackmail us," Trump said **in a post on Truth Social**, **adding that** the US was preparing to board and seize...' —— adding that 引出补充信息，是新闻写作中嵌入多层引语的标准技巧。

4. **条件虚拟句**："cast fresh doubt over whether it — **or any broader peace deal** — could hold" —— 插入语 "or any broader peace deal" 用破折号扩展讨论范围，暗示悲观预期从停火扩展到了整个和平进程。
